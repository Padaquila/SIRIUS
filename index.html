Not Financial or Legal Advice

Sirius is an analytical estimation tool designed to help users model real estate investment scenarios in Dubai. It does not constitute financial advice, investment advice, legal advice, or any form of professional recommendation.

All outputs — including acquisition costs, rental yields, capital appreciation figures, ROI estimates, and mortgage calculations — are approximations based solely on the inputs you provide. They are not guarantees of any future returns or outcomes.

Knowledge Required

This tool is designed for users who have a working understanding of:

  • Dubai real estate market dynamics, pricing norms, and transaction structures
  • Applicable regulatory fees including DLD charges, trustee fees, and agency commission standards
  • Mortgage products, LTV ratios, and financing structures available in the UAE
  • Off-plan vs ready property frameworks, payment plan structures, and handover conditions
  • Rental yield conventions, service charge structures, and property management norms in Dubai

Entering unrealistic figures will produce unrealistic outputs. Sirius does not validate the accuracy or market-appropriateness of the data you enter.

No Investment Recommendation
  • Sirius does not recommend, endorse, or suggest any specific property, investment, or financial decision
  • The tool does not account for individual financial circumstances, risk tolerance, tax obligations, visa status, or residency conditions
  • Market conditions, interest rates, rental demand, and property values are inherently unpredictable and subject to change
  • Past performance of Dubai real estate does not guarantee future results
Consult a Professional

Before making any investment decision, you should consult qualified and licensed professionals including a RERA-registered real estate broker, a licensed financial advisor, and where appropriate, a legal counsel familiar with UAE property law. Sirius does not replace any of these professionals.
